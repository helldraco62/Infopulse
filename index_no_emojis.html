Study Tips for Success 1. Create a Schedule: Break study time into focused sessions with breaks. 2. Use Active Recall: Instead of reading, test yourself regularly. 3. Summarize Notes: Rewrite in your own words to understand better. 4. Avoid Multitasking: Focus on one subject at a time. 5. Block Distractions: Use apps like Forest or turn on DND mode. 6. Teach Others: If you can explain it, you’ve learned it. 7. Use Spaced Repetition: Review older topics daily. 8. Try Focus Music: Lo-fi or instrumental music boosts concentration. 9. Sleep Well: Memory gets stored while you rest. 10. Stay Calm: Breathe, relax, and keep learning fun. Repeat. Improve. Achieve. — StudyPulse
